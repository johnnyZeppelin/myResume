Biography

Sifan Li is currently a graduate student pursuing the M.S. degree in computer science at Liaoning University, Shenyang, China, and a research intern in University of California, Merced, advised by Asst. Prof. Yiwei Wang. He received his B.E. degree from Shenyang Jianzhu University, Shenyang, China in 2021. His research interests include NLP, vision-language models, diffusion models, multimedia quality assessment, image processing, and computer vision.

Research Interests

Machine learning and systems, image and video processing, anything related to computer vision, efficient training and inference, multimedia quality assessment, AIGC, medical imaging, NLP, linguistics, etc.
